About Me

My Story

Hi, I'm Nathalia Clara, a bilingual UX Designer with a diverse background that enriches my approach to design. Originally from Colombia, I moved to the United States at 26 years old, bringing with me a rich cultural perspective and a passion for creating user-centric designs that bridge cultural and linguistic gaps.

My Experience

Currently, I work in Human Resources, where I leverage my skills in researching, problem-solving, and analytical thinking to improve internal processes and enhance employee experiences. My transition into UX Design was a natural progression, driven by my desire to blend my psychology background with my passion for technology and design.
